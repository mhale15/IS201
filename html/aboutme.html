About Me

Who am I?

Hi! My name is Madison (but you can call me Madi). I'm from Meridian, Idaho and I attend Brigham Young Unviersity studying Expereience Design and Management. I like to think that life is the ultimate experience and therefore we should fill it with meaningful and worthwhile things. I work hard and I play hard to create the kind of life I want to live.

I grew up in home with two hard working parents. My father is an entrepreneur and my mother is a doctor. One taught me to work hard and the other taught me that I can achieve anything I set my mind to. In my mind, that's the perfect combination. For as long as I can remember it has been one dream after the next and working to achieve the things I want more than anything. Now, my dream is to graduate with a degree in Experience Design and Management with an emphasis in expereiential marketing and venue management.


This is me!

I have been passionate about experiences since the first time I got on an airplane. Much of my desire to pursue experiences has come from the places, people, and cultures that have inspired me as I've traveled throughout the world. Becuase those experiences were so formative and intellectually enlarging for me, I wish to create experiences like the ones I have had for people all around the world!
